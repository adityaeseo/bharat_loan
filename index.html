A payday loan from BharatLoan is a short-term salary advance designed to cover essential, time-sensitive expenses. Rather than being a long-term loan, it acts as a bridge until the next salary credit.

Customers may apply for amounts starting from ₹5,000 and extending up to ₹1,20,000, depending on eligibility, income level, and internal assessment parameters. Repayment tenures generally range from 2 months to 12 months, keeping the obligation limited and easier to plan for.

Because the loan structure is straightforward, borrowers know:

  • How much they are borrowing
  • What they will repay
  • When each installment is due
